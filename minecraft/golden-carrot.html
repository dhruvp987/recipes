Golden Carrot

Image courtesy of MrJam003 under CC BY-NC-SA 3.0

A snack that is expensive but hearty.

Required Items

  • 9 golden nuggets
  • 1 carrot
  • Crafting table

Directions

  1. Open the crafting table.
  2. Place the carrot on the middle spot of the crafting grid.
  3. Place the golden nuggets on the rest of the spots in the grid, surrounding the carrot.
  4. Pick up the golden carrot from the crafted item spot and place it into your inventory.
